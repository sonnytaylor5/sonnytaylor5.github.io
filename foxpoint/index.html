Services

About

Fox point Roofing & Building services is a reliable, local, friendly and efficient company.
We are committed to providing a personal and friendly service to all clients whether domestic or commercial.

Based in the Portsmouth and covering the surrounding area, we believe in providing high quality roofing and building services at a fair price.
All aspects of external property services are covered.

It does not matter the size of the roof repair required, if it is a few tiles which need replacing or a major roof repair, we provide the same high quality service and guarantee.
As a result of the quality of our normal roof repairs and our emergency roof repairs we have earned an excellent reputation.

On Arrival at your property, we will assess what roof repairs are required, the scale of work involved and the materials needed for the roof repair.
We will then take the time to outline the problems and how we would intend to rectify the damage to your roof during a detailed consultation with you, we will also provide details of the costs and the work involved.
